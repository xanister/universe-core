Building Exterior Sprites (building_tavern.png, building_shop.png, building_residence.png)
=========================================================================================

These building sprites are composed from tiles extracted from the following
LPC (Liberated Pixel Cup) tilesets. All source tilesets are licensed under
CC-BY-SA 3.0 and/or GPL 3.0.

Source Tilesets
---------------

1. [LPC] City Outside
   URL: https://opengameart.org/content/lpc-city-outside
   License: CC-BY-SA 3.0 / GPL 3.0
   Artists: Tuomo Untinen, Lanea Zimmerman (Sharm), Guido Bos, Hyptosis,
            Johann C, Johannes Sjölund, Xenodora, William Thompson,
            Daniel Eddeland, caeles, bluecarrot16
   Used in: building_tavern.png (slate roof, brick walls),
            building_shop.png (red-brown roof, brick/stone walls)

2. [LPC] Windows & Doors (v2)
   URL: https://opengameart.org/content/lpc-windows-doors
   License: CC-BY-SA 3.0 / GPL 3.0+
   Artist: bluecarrot16
   Used in: All three buildings (windows and doors)

3. [LPC] Thatched-Roof Cottage
   URL: https://opengameart.org/content/lpc-thatched-roof-cottage
   License: CC-BY-SA 3.0 / GPL 3.0
   Artist: bluecarrot16
   Used in: building_residence.png (thatched roof, timber-frame walls)

Composition
-----------

These sprites were programmatically composed by extracting individual 32x32
tiles from the above tilesets and arranging them into building facades.
No original pixel art was created; all pixels originate from the credited
tilesets above.
