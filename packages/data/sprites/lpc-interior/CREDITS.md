# LPC Interior Assets Credits

These interior sprites are from the Liberated Pixel Cup (LPC) and OpenGameArt.org.
All assets are LPC-compatible: top-down orthogonal view, 32x32 pixel tiles.

Most of these sets are by **bluecarrot16** and designed to work together as part of the
[LPC Interiors collection](https://opengameart.org/content/lpc-interiors).
