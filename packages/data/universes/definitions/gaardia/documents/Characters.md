Pipras “Pip” Pennyroyal
Race: Elf
Gender: Male
Profession: Bard, Traveling Performer
Birth: 13 Álfsól 1422 4A
Appearance: Short blond hair, bright blue-and-yellow doublet, quick smile.
Personality: Irrepressibly optimistic, daring gambler, promiscuous, artistic soul.
Biography: Orphaned in Thornroot Forest (~1428 4A), adopted by Retsinax Burrowfoot (1430 4A). Tavern performer at 16, toured Muraii (1450-1471). Joined Matrim's Mistveil expedition (early 1472).
Key Events:
Late Summer (~14 Midskald) 1432 4A – Turquoise Tannery Caper, Hearthhome.
21 Stormvakt 1472 4A – Injured saving Rockwell from cougar near Fogfen Cross.
03 Solbrand 1472 4A – Killed ogre at Whispering Windmill.
