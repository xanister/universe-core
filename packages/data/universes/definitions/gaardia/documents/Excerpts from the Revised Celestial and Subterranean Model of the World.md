### ***CHAPTER III: ON THE UPPERMOST REALM — AHM'A, THE KINGDOM ABOVE AIR***

*“That which hovers beyond our stars has watched longer than we have walked.”*  
 *—Recovered fragment, Vault of Illuminus (Pre-Wrath)*

#### ***§1: On the Nature and Structure of Ahm’a***

*Long relegated to religious myth and apocalyptic verse, the region known as **Ahm’a** has, in recent decades, gained limited empirical confirmation through Rift-spyglass readings and recorded celestial anomalies. Ahm’a consists of vast **levitating landmasses** that drift high above the cloud-line, imperceptible to most ground-based vision save during **Solbrand eclipses** or divine portents.*

*These "floating continents" appear to defy gravitational law entirely. Their levitation is **not** accounted for by any known force from Rift engineering. Theories proposed include:*

* *The **Aether-Stone Hypothesis** (Magister Verril, 1399 4A): that Ahm’a rests on cores of pre-Wrath arcana-stone.*

* *The **Reversed Pulse Theory** (Rift Guild, 1412): that some inverted gravitational flow or “sky-tide” sustains their hover.*

* *The **Divine Suspension Theory** (Lantern School, conservative): that Ahm’a is held aloft by the will of Ahm the Lantern-God.*

#### ***§2: On the Inhabitants — The Winged Host***

*Multiple sources—including Seuran skyward cults and fragments from the Illuminan Codices—attest to the presence of **angelic or winged beings** in Ahm’a. Known variously as the Seraphim, Skyborn, or the Ahalani, these entities are said to radiate intense light and manifest flight even independent of terrain.*

*Some Rift-chronicles claim that they maintain rigid hierarchies of knowledge, form, and ritual—though no consistent envoy has ever returned from contact. It remains debated whether the Ahalani are a true race or a manifestation of some divine consciousness.*

### ***CHAPTER IV: LIMBO — THE INTERVAL BETWEEN SKY AND WORLD***

#### ***§1: Geospatial Properties***

*Between the heavens of Ahm’a and the surface of Anslem lies a chaotic intermediary region known in current cosmological literature as **Limbo**. It is not a continuous layer, but a **disrupted atmospheric field** that begins approximately three leagues above sea-level and extends indefinitely.*

*Travelers who pass beyond the clouds report:*

* ***Sudden gravitational reversals***

* ***Cyclonic air voids***

* ***Creatures of shifting form** or “mirage-beasts”*

* *Disorientation and hallucination, possibly due to altitude sorcery or flux-tide radiation*

#### ***§2: On the Creatures of Limbo***

*Several documented accounts, including the expedition led by **Sir Ven Kordall (1387 4A)**, speak of **“riftlings”**, **“featherworms”**, and **“sky-horrors”** with transparent bone and membrane, dwelling amidst the clouds. These beings appear to defy taxonomy, often behaving both as animal and air-current.*

*The Monastery of Hollow Winds in southern Muraii maintains an illustrated bestiary of Limbo denizens, though most entries remain speculative.*

---

### ***CHAPTER V: THE SURFACE REALM — THE WORLD OF ANSLEM***

*This realm is well documented in previous chapters. The surface world, home to human civilization, lies atop **a mobile crust** resting upon deeper, volatile realms. It contains a mix of bioregions, from the frost of the Shivering Wastes to the firefields of the southern rift zones.*

*Most notably, surface anomalies—such as the **Crater**, the **Floating Spires of Rimspire**, and the **Rift’s Breath Pulse**—may be partially explained by influence from **subterranean fluctuation** or **sky-tide instability**.*

### ***CHAPTER VI: ON THE LOWER REALMS — PANDEMONIUM AND THE FIRE DEEPS***

*“The deeper the torch descends, the louder the world groans.”*  
 *—Inscription above the descent shaft, Forge of Deep Hearthhome*

#### ***§1: The Caverns of Pandemonium***

*Beneath the crust of Anslem lies a vast under-network of tunnels and grottoes referred to collectively as **Pandemonium**. Not simply a mine, this region seems to obey **nonlinear temporal drift** and **gravitational inversion**. Explorers report:*

* *Time-loops (returning before they left)*

* *Water flowing upward*

* *Bioluminescent flora emitting melody-like hums*

*Dwarves of Hearthhome speak of the Living Walls, and halfling lore tells of the Forgotten Roads, paths which no compass may chart twice.*

#### ***§2: The Fire Deeps — Hellscape and Inhabitants***

*Beneath Pandemonium lies a still more hostile realm of lava-rivers and burning fens. This is the **Fire Deep**, sometimes called the **True Below**. Here dwell **demon-like fire-beings**, described in many traditions as the **Ifrahnir** or Children of the Deep Spark.*

*They are said to:*

* *Feed on heat and stone*

* *Speak in crackling tongues*

* *Manifest when summoned through bloodfire rites (see: Treatise on Infernal Evocations, banned 1305 4A)*

*Notable Event:*

* ***The Collapse of the Forge-Arch (1370 4A)** is believed to have occurred due to a Fire Deep breach.*

### ***CHAPTER VII: UNIFIED MODEL — THE WORLD TREE OR THE WHALE?***

*There are currently two major scholarly models for describing the whole of cosmology:*

#### ***1\. The Layered Sphere Model***

* *Adopted by Rift Engineers*

* *Anslem is a sphere encased in strata: Underworld, Mortal Crust, Limbo, Celestial Spheres*

* *Ahm’a floats in an aetheric band above the firmament*

* *Popular among Royal Mathematici*

#### ***2\. The Whale-Back Hypothesis***

* *Drawn from older murals and sea cult myths*

* *Anslem rests upon the back of a divine whale swimming the Night-River*

* *The sky-islands of Ahm’a are barnacles upon its back-breath; Pandemonium is its gut; Fire Deep is its belly*

* *Supported by some dream cults and Rimspire scholars*

### ***APPENDIX A: ON ILLUMINUS AND THE CELESTIAL FALL***

*The **Fall of Illuminus**, which marked the end of the 3rd Age, is now widely believed to have been the descent of a **floating city of Ahm’a**. Its plummet tore aether-rifts in the firmament and left scars such as the Crater and the collapse of the Temple Arches.*

*Recent analysis of surviving shards from the Illuminan spires suggests **pre-fall architecture resonated with both sky-magic and underrealm frequencies**, a trait not known in modern construction.*
