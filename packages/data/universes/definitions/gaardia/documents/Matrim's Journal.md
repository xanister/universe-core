### **Matrim's Journal – Mistveil Moors Adventure Log**

#### **15.03.1472 4A – Mistveil Moors**

* Met Pip, Sefton, Korr, Liam, Rockwell; journeyed to Mistveil Moors seeking work.  
* Sefton spotted an intriguing site for later exploration.  
* Made camp; encountered friendly ranger Eltanin Thornwhisper, exchanged stories and drinks.  
* Matrim's hunting attempt unsuccessful.

#### **21.03.1472 4A – Fogfen Cross**

* Uneventful travel to Fogfen Cross; secured lodging by performing music at local inn.  
* Matrim trained with sling-staff; Pip gambled, breaking even.  
* Accepted bandit-elimination contract; encountered friendly villager en route.  
* Defeated eight bandits; five killed, three fled. Rockwell severely injured.  
* Found valuable art in bandit camp.  
* Pip seriously injured protecting Rockwell from cougar; Sefton used downtime for training.

#### **30.03.1472 4A – Necromancer Encounter**

* Returned to Fogfen Cross; celebrated successful performance at inn.  
* Matrim recruited Ashina Majere temporarily to replace injured Rockwell.  
* Accepted contract from council member Eldon Quickfoot to rescue kidnapped Gaylen Mirador.  
* Tracked necromancer to ruins; stealthily eliminated skeletal guards.  
* Defeated necromancer, rescued Gaylen Mirador alive.  
* Returned and celebrated in Fogfen Cross.  
* Gambling temporarily banned (possibly related to Pip’s increased gambling in the region); crew recuperated, Pip taken to the healer.  
* No connections established due to Pip, their usual charmer and negotiator, being in the hospital and all negotiations currently being handled by Matrim.  
* In the news, an enemy is subverting the group’s reputation with the locals, spreading false rumors suggesting they were behind the kidnapping of Gaylen and faked the rescue.  The town hates the group.  
* A gambling craze breaks out, everyone wanting to gamble, just not with our merry band.  
* While both are recovering at the hospital, Pip and Rockwell spend an intimate night together.  
* A new friend, Bernerd, is made.  A friend of Gaylen who was nice to us and the only one who believed our rescue of Gaylen was genuine.  
* The locals finally come around on us, welcoming us back into the gambling halls and even doing some work for us.

#### **14.04.1472 4A – Minotaur Quest**

* Gambling resumed; Matrim conducted research at local library.  
* Pip fully recovered;   
* We wandered outside of town following the river and discovered a minotaur lair.  
* Sefton scouted ahead and stumbled across an unconscious adventurer who awoke and joined us, an immediate loyalty instilled.  They pledge to fight with us without so much as a name given.  
* The crew lies in wait for a time and a minotaur finally appears.  
* Several hits bounce off of the beast, especially those from Matrim’s sling and Pip’s arrows..  
* The minotaur charges Korr after one of his arrows penetrated the beast’s armor.  
* Rockwell, feeling a deep camaraderie with Korr, stays by his side.  
* Korr and the beast clash their weapons so hard, they send each other backward.  
* When the minotaur aims a blow at Rockwell, Korr steps in to absorb the attention of the beast and the blow itself.  
* Finally, one of Pip’s arrows penetrates and wounds the beast, causing it to cry out.  
* It continues attacking Korr, who defends magnificently.  
* Another one of Pip’s arrows penetrates and wounds, causing great damage to the beast.  
* Korr continually faces off against the beast wherein their weapons meet and they each knock the other back.  
* Pip shoots the beast in the neck with an arrow, causing a louder cry, but still it fights, charging at Korr with blood oozing down the arrow in its neck.  
* Pip shoots one last arrow that lands perfectly, striking the beast in its eye, blood spurting on Korr as the beast was closing in on another attack.  It falls back with a loud thud.  
* Pip and Korr breathe a sigh of relief, sharing a moment of camaraderie as they grip each other’s shoulders and exchanging knowing nods and smiles, appreciative of one another while the rest of the group cheers.  
* A moment of camaraderie, and light comedy, ensues between the soldiers when Sefton finds a new weapon among the loot, handing down his old blade to Korr, who passes his on to Rockwell, who passes hers onto their new ally.   
* Eventually, Pip plays a victory song with Matrim.  Korr feels so inspired as to lay down a beat on his shield.  
* Returned to town, celebrated as heroes.

#### **27.04.1472 4A – Journey to Veilstone**

* Met and befriended merchant Venecio.  
* Matrim earned coin through manual labor; Pip gambled.  
* Investigated goblin tracks; encountered goblins at overgrown ruin.  
* Looted ruin but forced to retreat after failed ambush.  
* Arrived at Veilstone, settled at Whispering Wyvern Inn.  
* Rockwell experienced sore shoulder, complained extensively.

#### **03.05.1472 4A – Ogre Contract and Fey Encounter**

* Explored Veilstone; pursued individual interests.  
* Pip secretly met representative from a clandestine group; accepted ogre elimination contract.  
* Matrim studied at library, limited success.  
* Located ogre at old windmill basement; planned ambush.  
* Liam impulsively attacked ogre, knocked unconscious.  
* Korr and Sefton engaged ogre in direct combat; Pip and Matrim attacked from a distance.  
* Pip killed ogre with arrow to the eye; acquired fine warhammer, ornate daggers, and quality armor.  
* Celebrated success at Whispering Wyvern; received payment.  
* Heard rumors of dark threat in Mistveil Moors.  
* Pip gambled extensively, breaking even again.  
* Matrim worked, earning additional coin.  
* Accepted Veilstone council task to locate a specific artifact (a wedding ring).  
* Followed lead to nearby forest; spotted mysterious fey creatures in quiet glade, decided to investigate further.

10.05.1472 4A \- Battle with spider worshiping fey and recovered treasure

* Snuck into glade and investigated  
* Found some tracks of fey creature and Matrim took it out with one shot from his slingstaff before it could alert the others  
* Party found the wedding ring hidden in the glade and escaped without a trace, headed back to Veilstone  
* The crew made it into town and were pickpocketed without seeing the culprit. They learned of a gang of sneaky thieves outside of town.  
* Went to the bar, and heard a rumor about a mystery at Moonmirror Lake reported by travelers.

11.05.1472 4A \- Crew goes to bring Richter to justice 

* Pip learns that Richter has a camp nearby. Korr, Sefton and Rockwell insist they go confront him and bring him to justice as he is their former commander.  
* Crew travels to where Richter is supposed to be camping  
* Sefton scouts ahead and reports that they are setup to ambush along with the Person that told Pip they would be here. The crew were setup  
* It is raining, so the crew wait for it to let up before they attack.   
* While waiting out the storm Pip and Rockwell go off alone and are intimate with each other  
* Much later that night the temperature drops significantly and the rain finally stops. The crew make their move.
